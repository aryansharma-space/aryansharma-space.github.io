About Me

Aerospace engineering with a long-range technical direction.

I am Aryan Sharma, an aerospace engineering student at the University of Colorado Boulder with a growing focus on guidance, navigation and control, spacecraft autonomy, astrodynamics, and spacecraft control. My goal is to build a technically serious body of work centered on rigorous analysis, strong engineering judgment, and deeper engagement with orbital and control-focused problems.

This website is intended to remain simple and focused. The homepage serves as a short introduction, while the rest of the site separates projects, resume material, and research writing into their own dedicated pages.
